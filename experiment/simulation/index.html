General Instructions

  1. Click on the components to start interacting it with in the connection area
  2. Hover over the raspberry Pi connections to get informations about various connections pin
  3. Double click on various connections points to make connections
  4. Double click on the empty space in the connection area to extend and bend wire connections

How to Connect

  1. Connect VCC pin of DHT11 sensor to +5 of Raspberry Pi
  2. Connect GND PIN of DHT11 sensor to GND of Raspberry Pi
  3. Connect the Data Out of the Sensor to the GPIO4, that is Physical Pin 7 of the Raspberry Pi
